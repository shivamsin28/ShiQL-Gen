About Us

Hi there! We're Shiql Gen, a friendly little platform born in 2025 from a group of final-year students who really wanted to make SQL less of a headache. Ever spent hours debugging queries or juggling between tools? Yeah, us too. That’s why we built this—to help you work smarter, not harder.

Here’s what we do to make your life easier:

Write Queries for You – Describe what you need like you’re talking to a coworker, and we’ll turn it into clean, efficient SQL.

No More Database Chaos – Connect to your existing databases, spin up new ones, or import data—all in one place. No more tab overload!

Tables That Build Themselves – Need a table? Tell us what it should do, and we’ll design it for you. Less time wrestling with schemas, more time getting things done.

Our Mission

We believe working with databases should be:

Simple – No more memorizing syntax or digging through documentation

Efficient – Less time debugging, more time building

Still a small team, still passionate, still eating too much pizza while we work on making Shiql Gen better every day. Come build with us.
